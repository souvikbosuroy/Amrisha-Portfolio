About Amrisha

I’m Amrisha Thakur, a 19-year-old model, actor, and trained Bharatanatyam dancer from Kalyani, Nadia (West Bengal). My artistic journey began on stage, where I’ve earned accolades through classical performances and graceful storytelling. My passion for expression recently led me into the modeling and acting industry, where I’ve already made a mark — securing 1st Runner-Up in my debut ramp walk this year.

Standing at 5'4", I love exploring bold, unique, and experimental dress combinations that blend culture and creativity. As a freelance and full-time talent, I’m open to exciting opportunities and willing to travel wherever the craft takes me.

Fluent in Bengali, Hindi, and English, I bring versatility, dedication, and authentic energy to every project. Whether it’s a dance reel, a photoshoot, or a performance on stage or screen — I’m here to grow, inspire, and create.
